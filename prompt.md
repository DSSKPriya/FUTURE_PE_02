# AI Content Marketing Using UGC Ads

## Prompt Engineering Framework for UGC Ad Generation

### Role

Act as a Senior UGC Content Strategist, Direct Response Copywriter, Consumer Psychology Expert, and Social Media Marketing Specialist.

Your task is to create high-converting User Generated Content (UGC) ad content that feels authentic, relatable, and trustworthy. The content should sound like a real customer sharing their experience rather than a traditional advertisement.

---

## Business Information

**Business Name:** The Brew & Bean Cafe

**Location:** Koramangala, Bangalore

**Business Type:** Cafe & Bakery

---

## Target Audience

- College Students
- Working Professionals
- Freelancers
- Families
- Coffee Lovers

---

## Objective

Create AI-generated UGC marketing content that:

- Increases brand awareness
- Attracts new customers
- Encourages social media engagement
- Drives more foot traffic to the cafe
- Highlights the cafe's atmosphere, coffee, and bakery products

---

## Platform Focus

- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Ads
- YouTube Shorts

---

## Ad Framework

Follow proven UGC advertising frameworks, including:

**Problem → Solution → CTA**

Ensure every ad clearly identifies a customer problem, presents the business as the solution, and ends with a compelling call-to-action.

---

## Content Style

- Authentic UGC
- Conversational
- Customer-Centric
- Conversion-Focused
- Social Media Ready

---

## Key Features to Highlight

- Freshly brewed coffee
- Fresh bakery items
- Cozy and aesthetic interiors
- Comfortable work and study environment
- Friendly customer service
- Perfect place for meetings, studying, and relaxation

---

## Deliverables

### Section 1: Audience Analysis

Identify:

- Top 5 customer pain points
- Top 5 customer desires

### Section 2: UGC Hooks

Generate 10 attention-grabbing hooks including:

- Curiosity Hooks
- Problem-Based Hooks
- Emotional Hooks
- Benefit-Based Hooks
- FOMO Hooks

### Section 3: UGC Ad Scripts

Generate 5 short-form UGC ad scripts optimized for Instagram Reels, Facebook Ads, and YouTube Shorts.

**Requirements:**

- Follow the Problem → Solution → CTA framework
- Suitable for Instagram Reels
- Suitable for Facebook Ads
- Suitable for YouTube Shorts
- Natural and authentic tone
- 50–100 words each

### Section 4: CTA Collection

Generate 10 strong Call-To-Action lines.

### Section 5: Social Media Captions

Generate 5 engaging captions suitable for Instagram and Facebook.

### Section 6: Customer Testimonials

Generate 5 realistic customer review-style testimonials.

### Section 7: Platform-Specific Variations

Create separate versions for:

- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Ads
- YouTube Shorts

for the best-performing UGC ad.

---

## Writing Guidelines

- Use an authentic UGC tone
- Sound like a real customer
- Avoid overly salesy language
- Focus on emotions and experiences
- Highlight benefits instead of features
- Create conversion-focused content
- Keep content concise and engaging
- Ensure all content is social-media ready

---

## Success Criteria

The generated content must:

- Feel like real customer-generated content
- Be ready to use in paid advertising campaigns
- Include multiple hooks for the same product
- Demonstrate clear conversion intent
- Follow the Problem → Solution → CTA framework
- Include platform-specific content variations
- Be reusable for similar businesses and brands
